# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 2026

This application is designed to respect your privacy and operate primarily on your device.

## Information Collection

Small Forge Software does not collect, store, transmit, share, sell, or otherwise process personal information or user data.

No analytics, advertising, tracking, profiling, telemetry, or data collection services are included in this application.

The developer does not receive, access, monitor, or store any user-created content.

## Data Storage

Notes, categories, settings, and other application data are stored locally on your device and remain under your control.

The application does not transmit your data to Small Forge Software or any third party for advertising, analytics, profiling, or marketing purposes.

## Android Automatic Backup

Android may automatically back up certain application data, including the application's database and settings, to the user's Google account through Android's built-in backup infrastructure.

This process is controlled by the user's device and Google account settings, not by this application or Small Forge Software.

If Android Backup is enabled on your device, your notes, categories, and other application data may be included in those backups. Small Forge Software does not have access to, receive, or manage this backed-up data.

Any data stored through Android's backup services is governed by Google's policies and terms. Users should review Google's privacy policy and account settings for information about how Google handles backup data.

## Manual Backup and Restore

This application may provide a Backup and Restore feature that allows users to create backup files of their data.

When using this feature, the user chooses the location where the backup file is saved, such as local device storage, removable storage, or a cloud storage provider selected by the user.

Small Forge Software does not receive, store, transmit, or have access to backup files created by the user.

Any storage provider selected by the user is governed by that provider's own privacy policy and terms of service.

## Permissions

This application requests only the permissions required for essential functionality.

Permissions are used solely to provide the features requested by the user and are not used for advertising, tracking, analytics, or data collection.

## Third-Party Services

This application does not include third-party advertising networks, analytics platforms, data brokers, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

This application is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults.
