# Small Forge Software

## Mission Statement

Small Forge Software creates simple, reliable, and enjoyable applications that are free to use forever.

Our software is developed solely to provide useful tools and entertaining experiences that enrich everyday life. We do not use advertising, subscriptions, in-app purchases, tracking, analytics, or other revenue-generating mechanisms.

We believe software should be straightforward, respectful of its users, and focused on its intended purpose. Our applications are designed to operate independently, without unnecessary complexity, distractions, or hidden behavior.

No permissions will be requested unless they are required for essential functionality. We strive to minimize the information and access requested by our applications and to ensure that users remain in control of their own devices and data.

Our commitment is simple: provide useful software that people can trust, use freely, and enjoy without concerns about advertising, privacy intrusion, data collection, or malware.

## Privacy Policy

Small Forge Software applications do not collect, store, transmit, share, sell, or otherwise process personal information or user data.

No analytics, advertising, tracking, profiling, telemetry, or data collection services are included in our applications.

Any information created or saved by an application remains on the user's device and under the user's control.

Applications request only the permissions required for essential functionality. Permissions, when used, operate solely on the user's device and are not used to collect, store, transmit, or share personal information.

Small Forge Software does not use third-party advertising networks, analytics platforms, data brokers, or tracking services.

This software is intended to remain free to use and free from advertising, tracking, and data collection. It exists solely to provide useful and enjoyable software that users can trust and enjoy without concerns about privacy intrusion, unwanted marketing, or malware.
